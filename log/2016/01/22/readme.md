# English
http://q-eng.com/diary/165

[自・他動詞](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=3T7Ih7kMMBw)

- 自動詞
直後に目的語をとれない動詞

- 他動詞
直後に目的語をとらなければいけない動詞

- 目的語
訳すと「〜を、〜に、〜へ」の「〜」にあたる名詞

*go = 自動詞*

  x: `I went the station.``
  o: `I went to the station.``

*have = 他動詞*

 o: `I had a pen.`
 x: `I had with a pen.`

arrive = 自動詞
I arrived at the station

reach = 他動詞
I reached the station
